Services

Bookbinding Services

Besides in-house conservation, binding and restoration, Scott is available to come to the location of larger collections in need of a collection conservation approach.

These onsite consultations may include recommendations for outsourced services as well as treatment proposals to be carried out in his studio. Insurance companies can also use these services to help expedite claims.
